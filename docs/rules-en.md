# Game Rules — Fantasy World Cup 2026 (Sport5 / Channel 5)
<!-- version: 1.0.0 — source: Sport5 official rules (Hebrew), translated June 2026 -->

Welcome to Sport5's Fantasy World Cup — a virtual football game that lets you assemble, manage and coach a squad drawn from all nations competing in World Cup 2026.

Each participant receives a fictional budget (detailed by stage below) and builds a 15-player squad (11 starters + 4 bench) that accumulates points based on real-world player performances. The goal is to maximise total points and outrank other participants and groups. The challenge lies in the scoring system, player selection and the constraints applied to each participant.

> Note: Sport5 / Dino employees may participate but are ineligible for prizes.

---

## Squad Selection

### Starting XI — must include exactly 11 players:

| Position | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Goalkeeper | 1 |
| Defenders | 3 – 5 |
| Midfielders | 3 – 5 |
| Forwards | 1 – 3 |

Formation must total exactly 11 and satisfy all min/max bounds.

### Bench — must include exactly 4 players, one per position:

| Position | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Goalkeeper | 1 |
| Defender | 1 |
| Midfielder | 1 |
| Forward | 1 |

### Players per national team cap (by stage)

| Stage | Max players from one national team |
|-------|------------------------------------|
| Group stage | 2 |
| Round of 32 | 2 |
| Round of 16 | 3 |
| Quarter-finals | 4 |
| Semi-finals | 7 |
| Final | 9 |

## Bench Auto-Substitution Rules

- Bench players count against the budget; if budget is insufficient, neither the bench spot nor the squad can be confirmed.
- Every participant **must** select all 4 bench players — fewer is not allowed.
- The bench must have **exactly one player per position** (1 GK, 1 DEF, 1 MID, 1 FWD). You cannot place two defenders on the bench.
- If a starter **does not play at all**, the system auto-substitutes the bench player of the **same position**. If all starters play, bench players score 0 (unless the All-Squad Points chip is active).
- The system only replaces with a player of the **identical position**. If two starters of the same position both miss out, only the first is covered — the second has no eligible bench sub and scores 0.
- During an open transfer window, starters and bench players can be swapped freely without counting against the weekly transfer allowance.
- A player whose national team has been eliminated and removed from the pool **cannot** be re-added to the squad in a later stage.

## Scoring System

Points reflect the real player's actual match performance.

### Playing Time

| Condition | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Played < 60 minutes | **+1** |
| Played ≥ 60 minutes (stoppage time excluded; extra time included) | **+2** |

### Goals Scored

| Scorer position | Points |
|-----------------|--------|
| Goalkeeper | **+7** |
| Defender | **+6** |
| Midfielder | **+5** |
| Forward | **+4** |

**Multi-goal bonus:** brace = +1 bonus, hat-trick = +2, four goals = +3 … generalising: n goals scored → +(n − 1) bonus points.

### Assists

| Event | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Assist (excl. own-goal assists, excl. rebounds off GK or woodwork) | **+3** |

### Clean Sheets (≥ 60 min played, excl. stoppage time, incl. extra time)

| Position | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Goalkeeper — clean sheet | **+4** |
| Defender — clean sheet | **+4** |

### Goals Conceded (GK / DEF)

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st goal conceded | Cancels clean-sheet bonus (no additional point deduction) |
| 2nd goal conceded onward | **−1 per goal** |

Extra time counts; penalty shootouts do **not** count.

### Penalties

| Event | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Won a penalty (incl. being struck/handled in the area) | **+2** |
| Caused a penalty (incl. handball) | **−4** |
| Goalkeeper saved a penalty (deflected off the keeper) | **+4** |
| Missed a penalty | **−4** |

### Cards

| Event | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Yellow card | **−1** |
| Two yellow cards → red | **−3** |
| Straight red card | **−3** |
| Yellow + straight red (same match) | **−4** |

### Own Goals

| Event | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Own goal | **−5** |

## Transfer Deadline & Lockout

Transfers and captain changes **lock 30 minutes before the first match of each round**. Any change made after lockout applies to the **next** round.

---

## Transfer Allowances by Stage

| Stage / Window | Transfers allowed |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Before round 1 (initial setup) | **Unlimited** |
| Group stage — each round | **3** |
| Between group stage and Round of 32 | **Unlimited** |
| Between Round of 32 and Round of 16 | **Unlimited** |
| Before Quarter-finals | **5** |
| Before Semi-finals | **6** |
| Before Final | **7** |

**Key constraints:**
- Cannot exceed the allowed number of transfers per round.
- Transfers do **not** carry over — unused transfers are lost.
- If a player you selected leaves the tournament pool, they score 0. Replace them in the next available transfer window.

## Leagues

Every participant is automatically placed in two leagues:

1. **Sport5 Overall League** — all participants.
2. **Favourite Team League** — based on the national team they selected.

### Private Leagues
- Any participant may create one or more private leagues and invite friends.
- Any participant may join leagues they are invited to.
- **Tiebreaker:** equal points → earlier registration timestamp wins.

---

## Captain & Vice-Captain

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Captain | Points (positive or negative) are **×2** and added to the total. |
| Vice-captain promotion | If the captain plays **0 minutes**, the vice-captain is auto-promoted for that round and their points are ×2 instead. One-time per round, applied automatically. |
| Triple-captain chip | Captain scores **×3** (instead of ×2) for one chosen round in the season. |
| Rotation | Captain / vice-captain can be changed each round before the deadline. |

---

## Bonus Chips (one use each per season)

Each chip can be used **once per season**. After confirming the squad with a chip active, **the selection cannot be changed**.

### 1. Triple Captain (`triple_captain`)
- Captain's points are multiplied by **×3** (instead of ×2) for one round.
- Can be stacked with Double Captains (see below).

### 2. Five Substitutions (`five_subs`)
- Make **5 transfers** (instead of 3) in a single chosen round.

### 3. Double Captains (`double_captains`)
- Both the captain **and** the vice-captain receive **×2** points for one round.
- **Stacks with Triple Captain:** captain gets ×3, vice-captain gets ×2.

### 4. All-Squad Points (`all_squad_points`)
- All **15 players** (starters + bench) score points for one round.

## Player Pricing

Prices are set purely for game balance and entertainment — they do not reflect a player's real-world value. Pricing is intentionally calibrated so that fielding a full lineup of superstars (e.g. Messi + Mbappé + Vinicius) forces severe compromises in midfield and defence.

## Worked Example

**Van Dijk (captain, Defender):** played 75 min, provided an assist, caused a penalty, received a straight red card; his team conceded 4 goals but 2 of them came after he left the pitch (one from the penalty he caused).

| Component | Calculation | Points |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Playing time ≥ 60 min | — | +2 |
| Assist | — | +3 |
| Caused penalty | — | −4 |
| Goals conceded while on pitch (2 goals → 1st cancels CS, 2nd = −1) | — | −1 |
| Straight red card | — | −3 |
| **Raw total** | | **−3** |
| Captain ×2 multiplier | −3 × 2 | **−6** |
